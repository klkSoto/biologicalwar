Biological Warfare, what is it?


The definition of biological warfare is basically the use of biological virus, bacterias and toxins in order to create chaos. This type of of of viruses and bacterias can be genetically modified in order to make it more fatal.

How it's used?


The bio weapons can be strategically used in differents points as said in un.org, " In addition to strategic or tactical military applications, biological weapons can be used for political assassinations, the infection of livestock or agricultural produce to cause food shortages and economic loss, the creation of environmental catastrophes, and the introduction of widespread illness, fear and mistrust among the public. "
